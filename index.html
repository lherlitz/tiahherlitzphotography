Welcome

My passion is to capture each unique personality, genuine connection and full emotion in the moment. For that reason I shy away from using too many props in order to keep the focus on YOU. To be able to look at the portraits on the wall and to be brought right back to that moment is just priceless. My goal is to truly capture those stages in life we don’t want to forget. Our littles are only little for so long, right? I’m an artist at heart and fully enjoy finding the beauty around me as well.

My name is Tiah, pronounced like Aunt in Spanish but with an “h” :)
I love coffee, punny puns and sunsets. As a traveling stay at home wife and a homeschooling Mommy to 3 energetic young boys I’m always looking for a reason to bust out my camera. 

I’m always learning, dreaming and growing my God given artistic gifts through the art of photography. Keep on scrolling for pricing info and reviews. I look forward to hearing from you.
