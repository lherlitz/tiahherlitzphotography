FAQ

Can I see a sample gallery?

ABSOLUTELY! Here is a direct link to a sample gallery just like the one you will recieve with your images. Feel free to click around and check out what's available in the online store etc. 

Do I have to order prints through you?

No you don't. However, to better guarantee professional quality printed items, it's highly recommended to order directly through your online gallery. It also helps to support my business which I greatly appreciate.

Can I extend my session day of?

Depending on availability, absolutely. I'm all about taking the time needed to fully capture exactly what we're going for.

Do you shoot weddings?

I mostly shoot small intimate weddings.  If you want more information on wedding photography we will chat about details over the phone or over a cup of coffee to see if I am the right fit for you. If not, I have others I will highly recommend. 

What does the + mean in the image count for each session?

Each session is guaranteed that minimum amount of hand touched high resolution images. If while editing I notice I have more to give I will do just that. These are your memories and I won't keep them from you or charge you an extra fee. This is an added bonus I enjoy blessing my clients with. Wether I get more or not depends on the cooperation of those involved while shooting the session. 

What if I need to reschedule?

I completely understand things come up and weather may not cooperate. Rescheduing is no big deal. We will simply work together on another time that works. I trust that we will both be respectful in communicating in timely manner.
